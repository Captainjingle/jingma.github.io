Brief Bio

I am an Assistant Member in the Division of Public Health Sciences at the Fred Hutch Cancer Research Center. Before coming to the Hutch, I was a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Pennsylvania, working under the supervision of Prof. Hongzhe Li and Prof. T. Tony Cai from 2015-2017. I received my Ph.D. degree in Statistics in 2015 from the University of Michigan, under the supervision of Prof. George Michailidis.
